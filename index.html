About Us

Located in Athens, GA, Golden Panda has been proudly serving the local community for over 10 years. Our chefs bring more than 20 years of cooking experience, and we focus on using quality ingredients to prepare fresh, flavorful dishes you can see and choose right at our hot bar.

We're proud of our friendly service and generous portions that keep our customers coming back. Stop by, grab a plate, and enjoy a variety of freshly made dishes. It's perfect for a quick lunch or a satisfying dinner.

Come taste the freshness for yourself. We look forward to serving you!
